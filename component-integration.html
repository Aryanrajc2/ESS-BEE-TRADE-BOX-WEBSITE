Component Integration

Seamless Assembly with Premium Brands

Component Sourcing Mounting & Wiring Interconnection Final Assembly

Bringing Panels to Life

At Ess Bee Trade Box, component integration is where the design and fabrication phases converge into a functional electrical panel. We meticulously assemble and wire high-quality components from trusted brands like Anchor - Polycab, Schneider Electric, ensuring optimal performance, reliability, and safety for every system.

Component Sourcing

Our commitment to quality begins with sourcing. We partner with industry-leading brands such as Anchor - Polycab, Schneider Electric, and L&K to procure all necessary electrical components, including circuit breakers, contactors, relays, and wiring. This ensures that every part integrated into your panel meets the highest standards of performance and reliability.

Mounting & Wiring

Following precise panel layout designs, our skilled technicians meticulously mount each component onto DIN rails or mounting plates. Wires are then cut, stripped, and crimped with precision, ensuring secure and reliable connections. Every wire is neatly routed and labeled according to industry standards for clarity and ease of maintenance.

Interconnection

This crucial step involves connecting all components according to the detailed schematic diagrams. Our team ensures that power circuits, control circuits, and communication lines are correctly and safely interconnected, adhering to strict electrical codes and best practices. This forms the functional backbone of the electrical panel.

Final Assembly

The integrated internal components are then secured within the powder-coated enclosure. This includes attaching the wired door, installing external controls, and ensuring all access points are properly sealed. The final assembly step brings all elements together into a complete, ready-to-test electrical panel.
